1. Logging In

Open MemberFlow in your web browser. You will see the login screen where you can enter your credentials.

Screenshot: Login page with username and password fields
  1. Enter your Username.
  2. Enter your Password.
  3. Click Sign In.

After successful login, you will be redirected to the Dashboard.

Forgot Password

If you forgot your password, click the "Forgot password?" link on the login page.

Screenshot: Forgot password page
  1. Enter the email address associated with your account.
  2. Click Send Reset Link.
  3. Check your email inbox and click the reset link.
  4. Enter your new password and confirm it.
Your account must have an email address configured for password reset to work. Contact your administrator if you don't have one set.

2. Dashboard

The Dashboard is your home page. It shows an overview of your mosque's key statistics and charts.

Screenshot: Full Dashboard page showing statistics cards and charts

Statistics Cards

At the top of the dashboard, you'll see summary cards with key numbers:

  • Total Members — Total registered members in the system
  • Active Members — Members with active status
  • Total Families — Number of registered families
  • Income This Year — Total contributions received for the current year

Income by Contribution Type Chart

A bar chart showing income broken down by contribution type (e.g., Monthly Contribution, Building Fund). The bars are stacked and split by currency when multiple currencies are in use.

Screenshot: Income by Contribution Type bar chart with year selector

Use the year dropdown in the top-right corner of the chart to switch between years.

Other Charts

The dashboard also displays:

  • Age & Gender Distribution — Grouped bar chart showing member ages by gender
  • Family Size Distribution — How many families have 1, 2, 3… members
  • Age Distribution — Bar chart of member age ranges
  • Gender Distribution — Pie chart of male/female/unknown members
Screenshot: Age-gender, family size, age, and gender charts (2×2 grid)

3. Members

The Members page is your main registry of all mosque members. Here you can search, view, add, and edit member profiles.

Screenshot: Members list page showing the table with search bar and status filters

Searching & Filtering

  • Use the search bar to search by name.
  • Use the status filter dropdown to filter by Active, Inactive, Deceased, etc.
  • Results update automatically as you type.

Adding a New Member

Screenshot: "Add New Member" form — Personal Information section
  1. Click the "+ Add Member" button in the top-right corner.
  2. Fill in the Personal Information: first name, last name, date of birth, gender, etc.
  3. Fill in the Contact Information: email, phone number, address.
  4. Click Save to create the member.

Viewing Member Details

Click on a member's name in the list to open their detail page.

Screenshot: Member detail page — Overview tab showing personal info and payment summary

The member detail page has multiple tabs:

  • Overview — Personal information, contact details, and status
  • Family — Family relationships and members
  • Finance — Payment history, obligations, and exemptions

Editing a Member

  1. Open the member detail page.
  2. Click the "Edit" button.
  3. Modify the fields you want to change.
  4. Click Save to apply changes.
Screenshot: Member edit page with form fields

Member Status

Members can have the following statuses:

  • Active — Current, active member
  • Inactive — No longer active but record preserved
  • Suspended — Temporarily suspended membership
  • Deceased — Passed away (with date of death recorded)
Clicking a member's name from other pages (e.g., Payments, Groups) will also navigate to their detail page.

4. Groups

Groups let you organize members into committees, classes, or any custom grouping. Each group can have its own roles (e.g., Chairman, Secretary).

Screenshot: Groups list page showing all groups with member counts

Creating a Group

  1. Click "+ Create Group".
  2. Enter a name and optional description.
  3. Select a type (e.g., Committee, Class, Youth Group).
  4. Set the max members limit (optional).
  5. Click Save.
Screenshot: Create Group form/modal

Group Detail Page

Click on a group to see its detail page with members and group information.

Screenshot: Group detail page showing member list, sidebar with info, and roles card

Adding Members to a Group

  1. On the group detail page, click "+ Add Member".
  2. Search for the person by name.
  3. Optionally select a role (e.g., Chairman, Treasurer).
  4. Set a start date and optional end date.
  5. Click Add.
Screenshot: Add Member to Group modal with person search and role picker

Group Roles

In the sidebar of the group detail page, you can manage group-specific roles:

  • Click "+ Add Role" to create a new role (e.g., Chairman, Secretary).
  • Set a max members per role (e.g., only 1 Chairman).
  • Roles show a usage count (e.g., "1/1" meaning 1 of 1 maximum assigned).
Screenshot: Roles card in group sidebar showing role list with usage counts

Editing & Removing Group Members

  • Click the edit icon on a member row to change their role, dates, or active status.
  • Click the remove icon to remove a member from the group.
  • Members with an expired end date are marked with a red "Expired" badge.
When a group has more than 5 members, a search filter appears to quickly find specific members.

5. Contributions

The Contributions section manages all financial aspects: contribution types, payment obligations, actual payments, exemptions, and member assignments. It is organized in tabs.

Screenshot: Contributions page showing the tab navigation (Contribution Types, Obligations, Payments, Exemptions, Assignments)

5.1 Contribution Types

Contribution types define the categories of payments your mosque collects (e.g., Monthly Contribution, Building Fund, Zakat).

Screenshot: Contribution Types tab showing list of types
  1. Click "+ Add Type".
  2. Enter a code (unique identifier).
  3. Add translations (name in each language).
  4. Toggle Required if this contribution is mandatory for all members.
  5. Click Save.
Screenshot: Add/Edit Contribution Type form with code, translations, and required toggle

5.2 Obligations

Obligations define how much and how often members should pay for each contribution type.

Screenshot: Obligations tab showing list with amount, frequency, and currency
  1. Click "+ Add Obligation".
  2. Select the Contribution Type.
  3. Enter the amount.
  4. Select frequency (Monthly or Yearly).
  5. Select the currency.
  6. Set a start date.
  7. Click Save.
Screenshot: Add/Edit Obligation form

5.3 Payments

The Payments tab shows all recorded payments. You can search by person name, filter by year and contribution type.

Screenshot: Payments tab showing the payment list with search, year filter, and type filter

Recording a Payment

  1. Click "+ Record Payment".
  2. Search and select the person.
  3. Select the Contribution Type. The currency will be auto-selected based on the obligation.
  4. If the type has a periodic obligation (monthly/yearly), select the period range. Multiple payment records will be created automatically.
  5. The amount is pre-filled from the obligation. You can override it manually.
  6. Set the payment date and optional reference (e.g., bank transfer number).
  7. Click Save.
Screenshot: Record Payment form showing person search, type selection, period picker, and amount
When recording a monthly contribution for a full year, select January through December in the period picker. The system will create 12 individual payment records.

Payment Receipt

Click the receipt icon (printer icon) next to any payment to open the Payment Receipt.

Screenshot: Payment Receipt modal showing mosque name, payment details, and action buttons

From the receipt you can:

  • Share — On mobile, opens the share menu (WhatsApp, email, etc.) with the receipt as an image. On desktop, downloads as PDF.
  • PDF — Download the receipt as a PDF file.
  • Print — Print the receipt directly.

Reversing a Payment

If a payment was recorded in error, you can reverse it instead of deleting it (this preserves an audit trail).

  1. Click "Reverse" on the payment row.
  2. Confirm the reversal.
  3. A negative reversal entry will be created and the original payment marked as reversed.

5.4 Exemptions

Exemptions allow you to exempt specific members from certain contribution types for a defined period.

Screenshot: Exemptions tab showing list of exemptions with person, type, and date range
  1. Click "+ Add Exemption".
  2. Select the person and contribution type.
  3. Set the start date and end date.
  4. Add an optional reason.
  5. Click Save.
When recording a payment for an exempted member, a warning will be shown indicating the active exemption.

5.5 Assignments

Assignments let you assign specific contribution types to individual members. This is useful when not all members are required to pay all contribution types.

Screenshot: Assignments tab showing assigned contribution types per member
  1. Click "+ Add Assignment".
  2. Select the person and contribution type.
  3. Set a start date.
  4. Click Save.

6. Currencies

Manage which currencies your mosque accepts for payments. You can activate currencies from the global ISO 4217 list and set one as the primary currency.

Screenshot: Currencies page showing active mosque currencies with primary indicator

Activating a Currency

  1. Click "+ Add Currency".
  2. Select a currency from the list (e.g., EUR, USD, SRD).
  3. Click Save.

Setting a Primary Currency

The primary currency is used as the default in forms and reports. Click the star icon next to a currency to make it the primary currency.

You must have at least one active currency before you can record payments.

7. Reports

The Reports page provides financial overviews for your mosque's contributions.

Screenshot: Reports page showing the payment summary table with year/language selectors

Payment Summary Report

Shows all members and their payments per contribution type in a table format. You can:

  • Select a year to view.
  • Navigate through pages for large member lists.
  • See totals per member and per contribution type.
  • Amounts are shown with currency codes when multiple currencies are in use.

Contribution Totals Report

Shows the total amount collected per contribution type, broken down by currency.

Screenshot: Contribution Totals report showing type totals with currency breakdown

Exporting Reports

Use the Export button to download the report data for use in spreadsheets or accounting software.

8. Import

The Import page allows you to bulk-import member data from external files.

Screenshot: Import page showing file upload area and import options
  1. Prepare your data file (CSV format).
  2. Click Upload or drag the file into the upload area.
  3. Review the data preview and mapping.
  4. Click Import to process the records.
Always keep a backup of your data before importing. Duplicate records are detected by name matching.

9. My Profile

View and manage your personal profile. If your user account is linked to a member profile, you can see your member information and payment history here.

Screenshot: My Profile page showing personal information and account details

Account Settings

From the User Menu (bottom of the sidebar), you can access Account settings to:

  • View your username and role.
  • Update your email address (required for password reset).
  • View your linked member profile (if applicable).
Screenshot: Account settings page showing username, email, and role

10. User Management

Manage login accounts for your mosque's administrators and members. Users are separate from member profiles — a user account is for logging into the system.

Screenshot: Users management page showing list of users with roles

Creating a User

  1. Click "+ Add User".
  2. Enter a username and password.
  3. Assign one or more roles (e.g., Admin, Treasurer, Member).
  4. Optionally set an email address for password recovery.
  5. Click Save.
Admin users typically don't need a linked member profile. Regular members who need portal access should have their user account linked to their member record.

Editing a User

Click on a user to edit their roles, email, or reset their password.

11. Roles & Privileges

Control what each user role can do in the system through the Roles and Privileges pages.

Roles

Roles define sets of permissions. The default roles are:

  • ADMIN — Full access to all features
  • TREASURER — Access to financial features (contributions, currencies, reports)
  • IMAM — Access to member and family information
  • MEMBER — Limited access to own profile and payment history
Screenshot: Roles management page showing role list

Privileges

Privileges are individual permissions (e.g., "View Members", "Manage Contributions"). They are organized by category:

  • Dashboard, Members, Family, Groups, Contributions, Currencies, Reports, Import, Settings, Users, Profile
Screenshot: Privileges page showing permission categories and toggles per role

To change what a role can do:

  1. Go to the Roles or Privileges page.
  2. Select the role you want to modify.
  3. Toggle permissions on or off.
  4. Changes are saved automatically.

12. Mosque Management

If you have organization management privileges, you can manage mosque details from the Mosques page.

Screenshot: Mosque management page showing mosque details

This page allows you to:

  • Edit your mosque's name and location.
  • Manage multi-mosque configurations (if applicable).
  • Switch between mosques using the mosque selector in the sidebar.
The mosque selector at the top of the sidebar lets you switch between mosques if your account has access to multiple organizations.

13. Language & Settings

Switching Language

MemberFlow supports multiple languages. To switch:

  1. Click the language selector at the bottom of the sidebar.
  2. Choose your preferred language (English or Nederlands).
  3. The interface will update immediately.
Screenshot: Language selector in sidebar showing English/Nederlands options

Changing Password

  1. Click your User Menu at the bottom of the sidebar.
  2. Select "Change Password".
  3. Enter your current password.
  4. Enter and confirm your new password.
  5. Click Save.
Screenshot: Change Password modal

14. Password & Security

Session

Your login session is valid for 24 hours. After that, you will be asked to log in again.

Logging Out

Click your User Menu at the bottom of the sidebar and select "Logout".

Security Best Practices

  • Use a strong, unique password.
  • Ensure your account has an email address set for password recovery.
  • Log out when using a shared computer.
  • Assign the minimum necessary role to each user.
