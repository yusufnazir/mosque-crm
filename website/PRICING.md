## Plans

### Plan: Free
- Label: Start Simple
- Description: Perfect for small groups just getting started
- Price: Free

#### Features
- 1 admin user
- Up to 20 members
- Basic member registration
- Member list & profiles
- Basic attendance tracking
- Contact export (CSV / WhatsApp-ready)

#### CTA
- Text: Get Started Free
- Style: secondary

---

### Plan: Starter
- Label: Organize Your Members
- Description: For small but growing organizations
- Price: $5 / month

#### Features
- Up to 2 admin users
- Up to 100 members
- Everything in Free
- Excel import (bulk members)
- Member search & filters
- Basic grouping (e.g. households, teams)
- Simple reporting (member count, growth)

#### CTA
- Text: Start Growing
- Style: primary

---

### Plan: Growth
- Label: Engage & Scale
- Description: Best for active and growing communities
- Price: $10 / month
- Badge: MOST POPULAR

#### Features
- Up to 5 admin users
- Up to 500 members
- Everything in Starter
- Member portal (self-service)
- Multi-currency support
- Advanced attendance tracking
- Communication tools (announcements)
- Priority support

#### CTA
- Text: Upgrade to Growth
- Style: primary-highlight

---

### Plan: Pro
- Label: Automate & Control
- Description: For larger organizations and advanced workflows
- Price: $15 / month

#### Features
- Unlimited admin users
- Unlimited members
- Everything in Growth
- Advanced reporting & insights
- Payment tracking (contributions, fees)
- Document management (upload & manage member documents)
- API access / integrations
- Role & permission management
- Data export & backups
- Priority + fast support

#### CTA
- Text: Go Pro
- Style: primary
